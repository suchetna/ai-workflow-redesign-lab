# Workflow Redesign Checklist

## 1. Define the current workflow
- What is the task?
- Who owns it?
- What tools are used today?
- Where are the bottlenecks?

## 2. Identify AI opportunities
- Which steps are repetitive?
- Which steps require synthesis?
- Which steps require judgment?
- Where can AI assist without replacing accountability?

## 3. Redesign the workflow
- What changes when AI is introduced?
- What new review steps are needed?
- What should stay human-led?
- What output quality checks are required?

## 4. Confirm adoption conditions
- Does the team know how to use the new workflow?
- Are there clear guardrails?
- Is ownership still clear?
- Can the workflow be repeated consistently?
